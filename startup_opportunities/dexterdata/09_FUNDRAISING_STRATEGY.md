## Investor FAQ (Be Prepared)

**Q: Why won't Scale AI crush you?**
A: Different business (annotation vs collection), robotics is niche/complex for them, we have 18-month head start + deeper robotics expertise.

**Q: What if simulation wins?**
A: Sim2real gap unlikely to fully close, we pivot to validation service, real data always needed for final tuning.

**Q: How defensible is this?**
A: Data flywheel + reputation + user network = 24-36 months to replicate. Moderate moat, but execution game.

**Q: Why can't customers build in-house?**
A: Break-even at 100K+ demos/year. For <100K, outsourcing to us is cheaper (no hiring, faster, higher quality).

**Q: What's the exit?**
A: Acquisition by Physical Intelligence/Skild/NVIDIA/Amazon at $300M-1B in Year 5-7. Or bootstrap to profitability.

**Q: Why are margins so high?**
A: Premium tier is software (90% margin), custom collection has scale economies, gig workers cheap vs US labor.

**Q: Customer concentration risk?**
A: Target 50+ customers by Series A, no single customer >20% revenue, multi-year contracts reduce churn.
