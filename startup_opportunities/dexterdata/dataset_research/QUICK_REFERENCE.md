## The Defensible Moat: Tactile Data

### Why Tactile is the ONLY Defensible Position

**Vision-based manipulation**:
- ❌ NVIDIA generates infinite synthetic data (near-zero cost)
- ❌ AgiBot provides 1M+ real demonstrations (free)
- ❌ 27+ datasets already exist
- ❌ Commoditizing rapidly

**Tactile/haptic manipulation**:
- ✅ Only 1 proprietary system (Sanctuary AI)
- ✅ Physics can't be fully simulated (contact, friction, deformation)
- ✅ Proven to improve performance (multi-modal > vision-only)
- ✅ Critical for contact-rich tasks (assembly, medical, food handling)
- ✅ Glove-based collection largely unexplored

### Evidence Tactile Matters

**From proprietary research**:
- Sanctuary AI: "Highest fidelity data" emphasizes tactile sensors
- Physical Intelligence: Multi-modal approach (vision + proprioception)
- NVIDIA: Synthetic + real = 40% better (real provides grounding)

**From academic datasets**:
- ContactDB: 187 citations (proves contact data value)
- ARCTIC: Contact annotations key differentiator

**Missing**: Quantitative studies proving tactile improves manipulation X%
**DexterData must**: Conduct empirical study ASAP
