## Key Insights from Batch 008:

### HIERARCHICAL METHODS (2022-2023):

1. **Hierarchical Policy Blending** (2212.01938): Optimal transport for policy composition
2. **Hierarchical Multi-Vehicle Planning** (2304.00843): Spatio-temporal hierarchical planning
3. **ASC** (2304.00410): Adaptive skill coordination
4. **Manipulator as Tail** (2305.01648): Loco-manipulation hierarchy

**Pattern:** Hierarchical approaches exist but mostly:
- Policy blending/composition (not waypoint-based)
- Multi-agent coordination (not single-agent)
- Loco-manipulation (not pure manipulation)

### WORLD MODELS (2022-2023):

1. **Multi-View Masked World Models** (2302.02408): For visual manipulation
2. **Multi-step Dynamics** (2305.02241): Multi-step predictions

**Pattern:** World models for manipulation emerging in early 2023, but not integrated with hierarchical diffusion

### MONITORING + RECOVERY:

1. **Decision Tree Monitoring** (2308.00944): Monitoring and recovery framework
2. **DoReMi** (found in batch 007): Plan-execution misalignment detection

**Pattern:** Monitoring and recovery is a theme but not learning-based + diffusion

### PRIMITIVE/SKILL-BASED:

1. **Prim-LAfD** (2212.00955): Primitive-based skills
2. **ASC** (2304.00410): Skill coordination
3. **Hierarchical Policy Blending** (2212.01938): Policy composition

**Pattern:** Primitive/skill abstraction common, but not continuous waypoints

### MPC + LEARNING:

1. **Enhancing Learning-based MPC** (2308.00570): Sample efficiency for MPC
2. **Efficient Deep Learning from MPC** (found earlier): MPC distillation

**Pattern:** MPC-learning hybrids explored but not with diffusion hierarchies

- HPB: Optimal transport for blending multiple policies
